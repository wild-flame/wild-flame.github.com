Brief Biography

LIN Dongwen is currently a Ph.D. student in Singapore University of Technology and Design.

He received his bachelor of Engineering in the school of Electronics and Information Engineering in 2014 from Harbin Insitute of Technology, China. He has been an exchange student to National Chiao-tung University, Taiwan in 2013.

He believes that technology changes our lives at an incredible paces, it leads us to a better life. He is interested in how technologies can interact with everyone and how they can benefit our daily lives.
