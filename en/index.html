Brief Biography

LIN Dongwen is currently a student in Harbin Insitute of Technology, China, and will obtain his B.E. in July, 2014. He has been an exchange student to National Chiao-tung University, Taiwan, in 2013.

He is not a professional coder but he find himself addicted to open softwares, especially to the open-source communities. He is trying to be one of those contributers who write those arts.

Besides coding, he is also a travel lover. He travels to find himself, to meet new people and to see the worlds. He thinks one of the most important things in travelling is communicating.

He also believes that technology changes our lives at an incredible paces, it leads us to a better life. He is interested in how technologies can interact with everyone and how they can benefit our daily lives.
